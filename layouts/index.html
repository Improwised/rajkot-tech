About

Do you love to read Books? Then you are in the right place. Welcome to the IT Bookworms Book Club. Let’s come together every month, meet book lovers like ourselves, pick a book, read it, share it, discuss it, learn new things and grow our and others’ knowledge by sharing what we learn.

Apparently, the practice of reading books creates cognitive engagement that improves lots of things, including vocabulary, thinking skills, and concentration. It also can affect empathy, social perception, and emotional intelligence, the sum of which helps people stay on the planet longer and happier.
